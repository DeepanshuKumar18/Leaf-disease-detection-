About This Project

Leaf Disease Detection is a smart AI-based platform built to detect diseases in plant leaves using deep learning and computer vision techniques. Our goal is to simplify disease detection for farmers, agronomists, researchers, and students. This platform combines the power of science and technology to prevent crop loss and improve agricultural productivity.

With just a single image upload or webcam capture, users can quickly detect plant diseases and get actionable solutions. Whether you're managing a small garden or large-scale agriculture, our app brings instant plant diagnostics to your fingertips.

üöÄ What We Do

We harness the capabilities of an ensemble of deep learning models (InceptionV3, ResNet50, and VGG16) to provide highly accurate predictions of leaf diseases. Our system has been trained on a diverse dataset of plant images, allowing it to differentiate between healthy and diseased leaves.

Our web interface allows users to either upload an image or take a live snapshot using a webcam. Once submitted, the system analyzes the image and provides not just the disease name, but also a brief description and potential treatments or preventive measures.

üí° Why It Matters

Plant diseases can lead to massive crop loss and food insecurity. Manual inspection is often time-consuming, expensive, and error-prone. Our tool automates and accelerates this process, reducing the burden on farmers and enabling more precise disease management.

By providing instant feedback, we help minimize damage, reduce chemical use, and support sustainable agricultural practices. Ultimately, this means better yields, healthier crops, and smarter farming decisions.
